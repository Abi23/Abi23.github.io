About Me

I have a masters degree in Electrical Engineering from Arizona State University, Tempe and a certification in Software Product Management from Product School, Silicon Valley. I am the founder of Esme hair, an online hair extensions brand. I have 7 years experience in tech industry, gaining experience in technical consulting, developing product line, launching a brand and customer experience. I am passionate about building products and providing services that are indispensable to customers.

I love companies and organizations that make an impact and give back to the community. I had an amazing experience being a role model at 10 Books A Home, a school that helps bridge learning gap among pre school children in a high poverty area. I ran the San Francisco half marathon in 2012 to raise funds for various grass roots projects managed by the Association for India's Development.
